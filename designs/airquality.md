# Air Quality Monitor

## Purpose
Measure environment, to figure out local air quality and environment.  On bad air quality days, it would enable us to know if the air quality at our house matches offical measurements.

Reports measurements to a central location. Possibly a custom service, grafana, at the very least. 

## Where to install
* Under eaves of studio
* Under front porch
* Behind shed near parking space
