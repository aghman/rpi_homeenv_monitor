# Software Defined Radio
Devices that could be connected to a pi to monitor radio frequencies

[RSPdx](https://www.sdrplay.com/rspdx/) - British made, expensive but well rated SDR. Can receive from kilohertz range to giga.

[Nesdr Nano 3](https://www.nooelec.com/store/nesdr-nano-three.html) - Tiny SDR, USB. cheap.
